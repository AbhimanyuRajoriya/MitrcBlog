About


MITRC Engineering College in Alwar is one of the best engineering colleges in Alwar MITRC offering various programs i.e. UG & PG in Artificial Intelligence and its specialization, Computer Science Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering and Mechanical Engineering, MITRC has established itself in Year 2007 and prove itself as a leader in technical education that makes it ranked one among top 10 engineering colleges in Alwar. Along with other engineering institutes in Alwar like Institute of Engineering and Technology Alwar, Laxmi Devi Institute of Engineering and Technology Alwar, Sunrise University, and NIET Alwar, MITRC has consistently produced successful graduates who have proved their excellence in various fields and accomplish the demand of society and industries. Thus, Our Alumini Success make MITRC as Top B.Tech Private College in Alwar. The college is known for its impressive placements and has seen students securing positions in top companies. MITRC is affiliated to Bikaner Technical University and Rajasthan Technical University(Old Batches), MITRC ensures that its students are well-prepared for the industry as well as for society. The college has been ranked as one of the top engineering colleges in Alwar and boasts a high cut-off for admissions specially in Computer Science Engineering and its Specialization. For those looking for the best college for engineering in Alwar, MITRC is a top choice. With its excellent faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and strong academic reputation, MITRC Engineering College is truly a top-ranking institute and Best Institute of Engineering and Technology in Alwar. For more information on admissions, fees, and course offerings, interested students can visit the college's website or contact the admissions office directly. With its commitment to providing quality education and putting students on the path to success, MITRC Engineering College is undoubtedly a top choice for aspiring engineers.

Department



Computer Science Engineering

Computer science is the scientific approach to computation, along with its wide range of applications. Computer scientists study the feasibility and mechanization of procedures, known as algorithms, which underlie the acquisition, processing, storage and communication of data.

AI and Machine Learning

From the start of August 2022, MITRC establishes a new department “Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning”. Its initiatives will definitely mould the students in such a way that they face the external world with prompt technical, interpersonal and problem-solving skills.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical power and systems are at the heart of modern society – and so too are electrical and electronics engineers. Engineers specializing in these fields have transformed the 21st century with technologies such as universal electric power systems, television, radio and medical imaging to name just a few examples.

AI and Data Science

AI and Data Science are the key enablers for disruptive innovations of this century, these emerging technological revolutions have reshaped our work and life style. From the start of August 2020, MITRC establishes a new department “Artificial Intelligence and Data Science”.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is the scientific approach along with its wide range of applications. Mechanical scientists study the feasibility and mechanization of procedures, known as algorithms, which underlie the acquisition, processing, storage and communication of data.

Civil Engineering

Civil engineering is arguably the oldest engineering discipline. It deals with the built environment and can be dated to the first time someone placed a roof over his or her head or laid a tree trunk across a river to make it easier to get across.

Applied Sciences and Humanities Department

Applied Sciences and Humanities Department emphasis to impart teaching and promoting implementation of practical aspects of Applied Sciences and Humanities to build a solid foundation as part of the Engineering Education.

Master of Business Administration

Master of Business Administrations Department has been established to Develop Managerial Skills, Business Communication Skills, Enterpreneur Skills and much more as part of the Management Education.
